6. Key Takeaways

Synthesized from Mordor Intelligence, Fortune Business Insights, Research & Markets, and GM Insights reports (2025).

🚀 Cloud is the Engine

At 18.2% CAGR, cloud storage will be a $342B market by 2030 — larger than the entire storage market was in 2024. AI/ML workloads and hyperscaler capex are the primary drivers.

💾 SSD Cannibalization Accelerates

SSD at 15.4% CAGR vs HDD at 4.4%. The crossover point where SSD revenue exceeds HDD by 2.3× happens by 2030 as NAND $/GB continues declining.

🧠 DRAM: Steady but Strategic

At $122B, DRAM is the largest "component" segment. HBM (High Bandwidth Memory) for AI accelerators is the growth hotspot within DRAM, with DDR5 adoption accelerating.

🏢 Enterprise Storage Goes Hybrid

On-prem enterprise storage grows at a modest 8% CAGR, but the real story is architectural: software-defined storage (SDS) and hyper-converged infrastructure now account for ~30% of deployments.

🌏 Asia-Pacific Leads Growth

APAC is the fastest-growing region, driven by China and India data center buildouts. North America remains the largest single market at ~42% share, but APAC's gap is closing rapidly.

⚡ AI is the Wildcard

Generative AI training and inference demand is creating a step-change in storage requirements — not just capacity, but low-latency, high-throughput architectures that benefit NVMe SSDs and HBM DRAM disproportionately.
